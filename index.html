Less is bore

This work involves discovering accidental
shapes that appear when basic shapes are
overlapped and designing them by composing a layout.
It was designed on b2 size paper,
taking advantage of the characteristics
of less is bore, in the form of a structure
created by overlapping three objects.

27th alphabet

This project is to identify the
characteristics of letters that
do not have an alphabet and devise them
to create a new alphabet.
They made the Korean sound "ㄸ" into
a new alphabet and gave it the name "Dopio."

Variations of logotype

This is a redesign of Hyundai Motor
Company's logo in line with its corporate philosophy.
The point is a modern and clean design that preserves the company's image
in line with the keywords of sustainability,
automobiles, technology and
innovation, and future development.

Two faces typography

This is a work of creating typography
that matches the two pictorial photos with different
feelings of model Choi So-ra.
The overall layout is in black and
white tones with elements of op art, and the typography
is arranged to match the movement of the person.
